# CDC National Environmental Public Health Tracking Network - Overlay Layers

This repository and its contents are provided by the **CDC’s National Environmental Public Health Tracking Network (EPHTN)**.

The information here is intended to support research, analysis, and public health decision-making. Please review and follow the disclaimer, terms of use, and data handling guidelines below.

---

## Overlay Layers 

The following Overlay Layers are available for use within the EPHTN Data Explorer:

[Surface Smoke ↗](https://mapservices.weather.noaa.gov/raster/rest/services/air_quality/ndgd_smoke_sfc_1hr_avg_time/ImageServer) – Near-surface smoke concentration from NOAA air quality models ([MORE INFO](https://airquality.weather.gov/))  
[Current Radar ↗](https://mapservices.weather.noaa.gov/eventdriven/rest/services/radar/radar_base_reflectivity_time/ImageServer) – Real-time weather radar reflectivity across the United States ([MORE INFO](https://radar.weather.gov/Conus/))  
[Transportation Noise ↗](https://onemap.cdc.gov/OneMapServices/rest/services/EHTN/CONUS_road_and_aviation_noise/MapServer) – National road and aviation transportation noise exposure data ([MORE INFO](https://www.bts.gov/newsroom/national-transportation-noise-map))  
[Active Cyclones ↗](https://mapservices.weather.noaa.gov/tropical/rest/services/tropical/NHC_tropical_weather_summary/MapServer) – Active tropical systems and cyclone summaries from the National Hurricane Center ([MORE INFO](https://www.weather.gov/gis/))
